About Me

Greetings! I'm Danielle Rodriguez, a Florida-based 3D artist known for my intricate hard surface modelling and captivating natural environments. I bring concepts to life by fusing artistry and precision, crafting awe-inspiring 3D creations that range from detailed structures to expansive scenes. My toolkit includes a variety of skills such as modelling, lighting, texturing, sculpting, and rendering. Beyond my technical skills, I am fueled by an insatiable curiosity, constantly adapting and learning to keep my work fresh and innovative. Collaboration is my heartbeat, as I thrive in dynamic environments where ideas meld. As a versatile 3D Generalist, I embrace challenges that fuel my growth. Proficient in Blender, Adobe Substance Suite, Unity, Unreal Engine, Adobe Photoshop and Lightroom, I seamlessly traverse from concept to visualization, creating everything from intricate buildings to breathtaking landscapes. Join me on this artistic voyage. Explore captivating designs, immersive environments, and dynamic scenes in my portfolio, where art and technology converge.
